Assessment content

Let me start by introducing myself; my name is Tyler Engle. I am a student at Keyin College currently in my second semester of Software Development. This is the first QAP of the semester: we are to create a webpage containing information on a species located in iNaturalist. As you already know, I chose to research The 'American' beaver.

The following are the required assessment problems:

  1. How many hours did it take you to complete this assessment?
  2. What online resources you have used?
  3. Did you need to ask any of your friends in solving the problems
  4. Did you need to ask questions to any of your instructors? If so, how many questions did you ask (or how many help sessions did you require)?
  5. Rate (subjectively) the difficulty of Making this all! from your own perspective, and whether you feel confident that you can solve a similar but different problem requiring some of the same techniques in the future now that you've completed this one.

The following are the required assessment answers:

  1. Time spent was roughly 3 hours of research, 1-2 hours writing up the essay, and somewhere between 7-10 hours working on the webpage; I worked on it for 3 seperate session of about 3 hours each.
  2. Online resources used were w3 schools, and class notes.
  3. I did not need to ask my peers for help, however, I did have a meeting with Scott where we worked on our projects and bounced brainwaves back and forth.
  4. I asked my instructor if I need to replace all symbols with ASCII code.
  5. I did not find this project particularly difficult; doing up the webpage was the easy part. I feel that I have a good understanding of HTML syntax. The difficult part for me was obtaining and sorting the information, paraphrasing it, and creating a summarizing essay.
